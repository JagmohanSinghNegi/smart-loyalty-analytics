How It Works

The loyalty prediction model analyzes customer behavior using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) metrics:

  • Recency: How recently the customer made a purchase
  • Frequency: How often the customer purchases
  • Monetary: How much the customer spends

A score closer to 1.0 indicates higher loyalty likelihood.
